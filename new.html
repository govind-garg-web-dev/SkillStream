The Virtual Economy

A fair economy where
knowledge has real value.

SkillStream runs on a transparent two-currency system. Learners spend Coins. Educators earn Diamonds. Cash out real money — it's that simple.

💰

You Buy Coins

Purchase Coin packs with real money via Stripe, Apple Pay, or Google Pay. Secure, fast, and straightforward.

↓
🎁

Spend on Sessions & Gifts

Use Coins for private 1:1 calls (billed per minute) or send virtual gifts in live streams to creators you love.

↓
💎

Educators Earn Diamonds

Every Coin spent converts to Diamonds in the educator's wallet. Diamonds are real, cashable earnings.

↓
🏦

Cash Out Anytime

Once a minimum Diamond threshold is reached, educators withdraw directly to their bank account via Stripe payouts.

Escrow-Protected Calls

When a 1:1 call begins, your Coins are held in secure escrow. The system deducts only for time used. If Coins run low, you get a warning and can top up — the call never drops without notice.

Set Your Own Rate

Educators choose their per-minute Coin rate. Whether you're a rising coach or a world-class consultant, your rate reflects your value. Change it any time.

Transparent Platform Fee

SkillStream takes a small, clearly disclosed percentage on every transaction. No hidden cuts, no surprise deductions. Your dashboard always shows exactly what you earned and what was taken.

ACID-Compliant Transactions

Every Coin deduction, Diamond credit, and payout is protected by enterprise-grade database guarantees. Your money never disappears into a technical error.

Safety & Trust

A platform you can
trust completely.

SkillStream is built with uncompromising safety standards. Every stream. Every session. Every time.

🤖 Real-Time AI Moderation

Every live stream is continuously scanned by AI for inappropriate visuals or audio. Violations trigger instant stream termination and automatic account suspension — before humans even need to act.

✅ Verified Educators Only

Before anyone can teach on SkillStream, they must pass a full identity verification (KYC) process. No anonymous bad actors. No fake gurus. Every educator is a real, verified person.

🚨 One-Tap Reporting

A prominent report button is always visible in every stream and chat. Reports are investigated immediately. If a threshold is crossed, streams are auto-terminated and the case is escalated to a human reviewer.

🔐 Bank-Grade Financial Security

All payments are processed through Stripe with full PCI-DSS compliance. No card data ever touches our servers. Transactions are protected by ACID database guarantees — your money is always safe.
